About Me

I'm a senior undergraduate studying Mathematics-Physics Fundamental Science (Artificial Intelligence Specialization) at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). In particular, I'm a member of Yingcai Honors College of UESTC.

I'm a member of Data Intelligence Group, UESTC, supervised by Profs. Wen Li and Lixin Duan. Besides, I currently receive remote guidance from Postdoc Bo Wu in MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab.

I focus on problems in machine learning and computer vision, where beautiful theory and practical methodology meet. My current research interests include but are not limited to 1) video understanding, 2) causal inference, and 3) adversarial attack.
